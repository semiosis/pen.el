Ionic compounds are made up of:
- metals
- nonmetals

Molecular compounds are made up only of:
- nonmetals

The nonmetals are on the far right and top-
right corner of the periodic table.

Except, hydrogen is the only exception outside of the
nonmetal triangle which is also regarded as a nonmetal.
