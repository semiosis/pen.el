Berry vils should finish up right around clicking
castle. Send them to wood.

Deer vils to gold.

Get axe, horse collar, and fletching.

When you hit castle, first make a castle with the
stone vils. If you don't have enough stone for a 2nd
castle, then keep 2-3 working. When you have the
required stone, take all your gold vils and have
them build the 2nd castle.

After the stone vils finish, leave 2 on stone and
send the rest to wood/gold, as you see fit. You
should be able to queue plumes almost non-stop and
keep your TC running.

Note: In feudal you are susceptible to scout rushes,
so have your flank send spears to your gold and
stone and maybe deer if he's very nice.

Good luck and have fun!
