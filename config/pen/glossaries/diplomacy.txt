envoy
    A diplomatic agent.
    
    Any accredited messenger or
    representative.
    
    Also called envoy extraordinary, minister
    plenipotentiary.
    
    A diplomatic agent of the second rank,
    next in status after an ambassador.

plenipotentiary
    A person, especially a diplomat, invested
    with the full power of independent action
    on behalf of their government, typically
    in a foreign country.
