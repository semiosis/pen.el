satire
    The use of humour, irony, exaggeration, or
    ridicule to expose and criticize people's
    stupidity or vices, particularly in the
    context of contemporary politics and other
    topical issues.
    "the crude satire seems to be directed at
    the fashionable protest singers of the
    time"

primary genres
    The primary genres in literature are
    poetry, drama/play, essay, short story,
    and novel.
    
    The term genre is used quite often to
    denote literary sub-classifications or
    specific types of literature such as
    comedy, tragedy, epic poetry, thriller,
    science fiction, romance, etc.
