noble lie
    [#politics]

    A noble lie is a myth or a lie typically
    of religious nature, knowingly propagated
    by an elite to maintain social harmony or
    advance an agenda.
    
    The noble lie is a concept originated by
    Plato as described in The Republic.

    https://youtu.be/jLesc5lITvo?t=223

    The noble lie that he proposed is that we
    are all born with gold, silver or a
    mixture of brass and iron in our souls,
    which determines our roles in life.
