Les Troyens
    Opera by Hector Berlioz.

    Les Troyens is a French grand opera in
    five acts by Hector Berlioz. The libretto
    was written by Berlioz himself from
    Virgil's epic poem the Aeneid; the score
    was composed between 1856 and 1858.
