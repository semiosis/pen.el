Francophilia
    A person who has a strong affinity towards
    any or all of the French language, French
    history, French culture or French people.
    
    That affinity may include France itself or
    its history, language, cuisine,
    literature, etc.

Beaujolais
    A type of red wine that comes from the
    region of eastern France called
    Beaujolais.

    "...a fruity Beaujolais."
