weiqi
    An abstract strategy board game for two
    players in which the aim is to surround
    more territory than the opponent.

    The game was invented in China more than
    2,500 years ago and is believed to be the
    oldest board game continuously played to
    the present day.

    Literal meaning:
    - 'encirclement board game'
