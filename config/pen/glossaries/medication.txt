Ivermectin
    A medication used to treat parasite
    infestations.
    
    In humans, these include head lice,
    scabies, river blindness,
    strongyloidiasis, trichuriasis,
    ascariasis, and lymphatic filariasis.
    
    In veterinary medicine, the medication is
    used to prevent and treat heartworm and
    acariasis, among other indications.
