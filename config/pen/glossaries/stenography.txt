Stenography
    The art of stenography has existed for
    over a century, and has been computerized
    since the late twentieth century.
    
    All over the world, professional
    stenographers write at realtime speeds in
    order to take depositions and caption live
    television.
    
    Plover brings this powerful technology to
    anyone who is willing to learn it.
