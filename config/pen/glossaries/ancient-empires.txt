Parthia
Parthian Empire
    The Parthian Empire, also known as the
    Arsacid Empire, was a major Iranian
    political and cultural power in ancient
    Iran from 247 BC to 224 AD.

Hyrcania
    A historical region composed of the land
    south-east of the Caspian Sea in modern-
    day Iran and Turkmenistan, bound in the
    south by the Alborz mountain range and the
    Kopet Dag in the east.
