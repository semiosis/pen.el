IaaS
Infrastructure as a service
    A form of cloud computing that provides
    virtualized computing resources over the
    internet.
    
    IaaS is one of the three main categories
    of cloud computing services, alongside
    software as a service (SaaS) and platform
    as a service (PaaS).
