differential
    A gear train with three shafts that has
    the property that the rotational speed of
    one shaft is the average of the speeds of
    the others, or a fixed multiple of that
    average.

pneumatics
    A branch of engineering that makes use of
    gas or pressurized air.
    
    Pneumatic systems used in industry are
    commonly powered by compressed air or
    compressed inert gases.
    
    A centrally located and electrically-
    powered compressor powers cylinders, air
    motors, pneumatic actuators, and other
    pneumatic devices.
