schema
    [#social science]

    Mental structures that an individual uses
    to organize knowledge and guide cognitive
    processes and behaviour.

schemata
    [#social science]

    The plural of schema. 

    Categorize objects and events based on
    common elements and characteristics and
    thus interpret and predict the world.

Caste
    A form of social stratification
    characterised by endogamy, hereditary
    transmission of a style of life which
    often includes an occupation, ritual
    status in a hierarchy, and customary
    social interaction and exclusion based on
    cultural notions of purity and pollution.
