OpenSearch
    https://opensearch.org/faq/

    A fully open source search and analytics
    suite.

    A fork of ElasticSearch 7.10.

    OpenSearch includes OpenSearch (derived
    from Elasticsearch 7.10.2) and OpenSearch
    Dashboards (derived from Kibana 7.10.2).
    
    OpenSearch is the new home for the plugins
    and advanced features distributed with
    Open Distro for Elasticsearch.
    
    Open Distro for Elasticsearch will end
    with version 1.13
