Titleist
    An American brand name of golf equipment
    produced by the Acushnet Company,
    headquartered in Fairhaven, Massachusetts,
    United States.
    
    The Titleist brand, established in 1932 by
    Philip E. Young, focuses on golf balls and
    clubs.
    
    The name Titleist is derived from the word
    "titlist", which means "title holder".
