Wassily Kandinsky
Wassily Wassilyevich Kandinsky
    [Russian painter]

    Was a Russian painter and art theorist.
    
    Kandinsky is generally credited as the
    pioneer of abstract art.
    
    Born in Moscow, Kandinsky spent his
    childhood in Odessa, where he graduated at
    Grekov Odessa Art school.
    
    He enrolled at the University of Moscow,
    studying law and economics.
