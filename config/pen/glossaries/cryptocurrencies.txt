Polkadot
    A network protocol that allows arbitrary
    data—not just tokens—to be transferred
    across blockchains.

decentralized autonomous organization
DAO
    An entity with no central leadership.
    
    Decisions get made from the bottom-up,
    governed by a community organized around a
    specific set of rules enforced on a
    blockchain.
    
    A DAO works without hierarchical
    management and can have a large number of
    purposes.
