microservices
microservice architecture
    [architectural style]
    
    Structures an application as a collection
    of services that are:
    - Highly maintainable and testable
    - Loosely coupled
    - Independently deployable
    - Organized around business capabilities
    - Owned by a small team

    Enables the rapid, frequent and reliable
    delivery of large, complex applications.
    
    It also enables an organization to evolve
    its technology stack.

Service-oriented architecture
    A style of software design where services
    are provided to the other components by
    application components, through a
    communication protocol over a network.
