transubstantiation
    According to the teaching of the Catholic
    Church, "the change of the whole substance
    of bread into the substance of the Body of
    Christ and of the whole substance of wine
    into the substance of the Blood of Christ.

magisterium
The magisterium of the Catholic Church
    The church's authority or office to give
    authentic interpretation of the Word of
    God, "whether in its written form or in
    the form of Tradition."
