    Estimated Life: 5 - 64/65 AD

    As a tent maker, he worked with leather,
    and leatherwork is not noisy.
    
    While he worked, therefore, he could have
    talked, and once he was found to have
    something interesting to say, people would
    have dropped by from time to time to
    listen.
    
    It is very probable that Paul spread the
    gospel in this way.g

    A Christian apostle who spread the
    teachings of Jesus in the first-century
    world.
    
    Generally regarded as one of the most
    important figures of the Apostolic Age, he
    founded several Christian communities in
    Asia Minor and Europe from the mid-40s to
    the mid-50s AD.
