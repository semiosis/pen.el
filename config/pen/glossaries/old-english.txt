wherefore
    Could take any of these meanings:
    - for what reason
    - why
    - Consequently,

whence
    - Question from what place or source.
    - Describe where. from which; from where.

whither
    - Question to what place or state.
      "whither are we bound?"
    - Describe to which (with reference to a place).
      "the barbecue had been set up by the lake, whither Matthew and Sara were conducted"

cierring
    turning

cwic

    "the quick and the dead"
