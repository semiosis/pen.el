churn rate
    [#saas]

    The number of subscribers who discontinued
    their subscription during a period divided
    into the total number of subscribers.

Playbooks
    This seems to be a term often used when
    talking about 'scripts' created through a
    GUI.

    GitGuardian
        Playbooks enable you to customize and
        automate GitGuardian behavior.
        
        This allows users to focus on
        resolving incidents and spending less
        time on investigations.
        
        By default, GitGuardian offers the
        Auto-healing playbook to automate the
        process of sharing the incident with
        the developer involved.
