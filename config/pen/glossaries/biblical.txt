Pharisees
    A Jewish social movement and a school of
    thought in the Levant during the time of
    Second Temple Judaism.
    
    After the destruction of the Second Temple
    in 70 CE, Pharisaic beliefs became the
    foundational, liturgical, and ritualistic
    basis for Rabbinic Judaism.
