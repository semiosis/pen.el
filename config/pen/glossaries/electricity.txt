rectenna
    A rectifying antenna.

    A special type of receiving antenna that
    is used for converting electromagnetic
    energy into direct current (DC)
    electricity.

    They are used in wireless power
    transmission systems that transmit power
    by radio waves.

voltage
    The difference in charge between two
    points.

current
    The rate at which charge is flowing.

resistance
    A material's tendency to resist the flow
    of charge (current).
