citizen science
community science
crowd science
crowd-sourced science
civic science
volunteer monitoring
    Scientific research conducted, in whole or
    in part, by amateur (or nonprofessional)
    scientists.
    
    Citizen science is sometimes described as
    "public participation in scientific
    research", participatory monitoring, and
    participatory action research whose
    outcomes are often advancements in
    scientific research by improving the
    scientific community's capacity, as well
    as increasing the public's understanding
    of science.
