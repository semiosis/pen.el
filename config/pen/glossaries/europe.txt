Belgium
    [Country in Europe]

    A country in Western Europe, is known for
    medieval towns, Renaissance architecture
    and as headquarters of the European Union
    and NATO.
    
    The country has distinctive regions
    including Dutch-speaking Flanders to the
    north, French-speaking Wallonia to the
    south and a German-speaking community to
    the east.
    
    The bilingual capital, Brussels, has
    ornate guildhalls at Grand-Place and
    elegant art-nouveau buildings.
