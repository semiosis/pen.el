    A medical professional who practices
    veterinary medicine.
    
    They manage a wide range of health
    conditions and injuries in non-human
    animals.
    
    Along with this, vets also play a role in
    animal reproduction, animal health
    management, conservation, husbandry and
    breeding and preventive medicine like
    animal nutrition, vaccination and
    parasitic control as well as biosecurity
    and zoonotic disease surveillance and
    prevention.
