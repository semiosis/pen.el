síneadh fada
    Literally means "a long stretch".
    Means: lenition.

    https://youtu.be/_nxu1CssWLk?t=67

    https://youtu.be/0lOyhKf0sdM?t=75

    Each of the vowels has the capacity to be
    doubled by putting a síneadh fada on top.

    Síneadh fada literally means "a long
    stretch" and when a síneadh fada occurs
    over a vowel it shows that the vowel is
    long rather than short.

fada
    Means: long.

    It is commonly used for síneadh fada,
    which is a diacritic sign similar to acute
    accent: á é ó ú í. It signifies a long (or
    historically long) vowel.
