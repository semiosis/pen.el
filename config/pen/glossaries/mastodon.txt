Home
    Posts from people you follow will appear
    in your home feed.
    
    You can follow anyone on any server!

Local
    Public posts from people on the same
    server as you will appear in the local
    timeline.

Federated
    Public posts from other servers of the
    fediverse will appear in the federated
    timeline.

Reply
    You can reply to other people's and your
    own toots, which will chain them together
    in a conversation.

Boost
    You can share other people's toots with
    your followers by boosting them.

Favourite
    You can save a toot for later, and let the
    author know that you liked it, by
    favouriting it.
