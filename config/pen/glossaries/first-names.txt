Megan
    pearl

    Strong, capable; Pearl.

    A diminutive of Margaret from the Greek
    'margarites' meaning 'pearl'.

    Its Greek roots can also mean 'great' and
    'mighty'.

Laria
    Greek origin.

    The meaning of Laria is "the stars are
    mine".

Amelia
    Latin origin.

    The root of this name is Amal, which means
    "work." In this case, when translated,
    "work" refers to industriousness and
    fertility rather than labor.
