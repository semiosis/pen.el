reionization
    [#physical cosmology]
    [#Big Bang]

    The process that caused matter in the
    universe to reionize after the lapse of
    the "dark ages".
    
    Reionization is the second of two major
    phase transitions of gas in the universe.

    https://aibhleog.github.io/ksmposter.html

photoionization
    The physical process in which an ion is
    formed from the interaction of a photon
    with an atom or molecule.
