                            <tr>
                                <td rowspan="3" class="number">4.</td>
                                <td nowrap="nowrap">Q.</td>
                                <td><h2><div class="line">What does God's law require of us?</div>
</h2></td>
                            </tr>
                            <tr>
                                <td nowrap="nowrap">A.</td>
                                <td><div class="line">Christ teaches us this in a summary in Matthew 22:</div>
<div class="line indented"><i>Love the Lord your God</i></div>
<div class="line indented"><i>with all your heart</i></div>
<div class="line indented"><i>and with all your soul</i></div>
<div class="line indented"><i>and with all your mind.</i> <sup><a rel="footnote" href="#fn-4-1">1</a></sup></div>
<div class="line indented"><i>This is the first and greatest commandment.</i></div>
<div class="line indented"><i>And the second is like it:</i></div>
<div class="line indented"><i>Love your neighbour as yourself.</i></div>
<div class="line indented"><i>All the Law and the Prophets hang on these</i></div>
<div class="line indented"><i>two commandments.</i> <sup><a rel="footnote" href="#fn-4-2">2</a></sup></div>
</td>
                            </tr>
                            <tr>
                                <td></td>
                                <td class="ld-footnotes"><ul class="footnotes">
<li id="fn-4-1"><span class="number">1.</span> Deut 6:5.</li>
<li id="fn-4-2"><span class="number">2.</span> Lev 19:18.</li>
</ul></td>
                            </tr>
                        </table>

</td>

                                <td class="ld-footnotes"><ul class="footnotes">
