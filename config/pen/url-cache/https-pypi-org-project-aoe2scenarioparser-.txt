            <h1>AoE2ScenarioParser</h1>
<p>This is a project for editing parts of an <code>aoe2scenario</code> file from <strong>Age of Empires 2 Definitive Edition</strong> outside
the in-game editor.</p>
<h1>Getting started</h1>
