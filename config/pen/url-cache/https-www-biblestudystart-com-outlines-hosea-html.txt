  <td class="verses">Ho 4:12-14.</td>
  <td class="summary">He exposes the ignorance and wickedness of the priests, and profligacy of the people, he will leave their wives and daughters to commit lewdness, without present punishment.</td>
 </tr>
 <tr>
  <td class="verses">Ho 4:15-19.</td>
  <td class="summary">He warns Judah, not to imitate Israel's crimes, which are still further reproved.</td>

 <tr>

 </tr>
 <tr>
  <td class="verses">Ho 5:15.</td>
  <td class="summary">An intimation is given of mercy on their repentance.</td>
 </tr>

 <tr>
  <td class="verses">Ho 6:1-3.</td>

 </tr>
 <tr>
  <td class="verses">Ho 6:4.</td>
  <td class="summary">A lamentation over those who had sinned after conviction.</td>
 </tr>
 <tr>
  <td class="verses">Ho 6:5-11.</td>
  <td class="summary">Reproofs of obstinate sinners, and threatenings against them.</td>
 </tr>

 <tr>

  <td class="summary">A reproof of manifold sins.</td>
 </tr>
 <tr>
  <td class="verses">Ho 7:11-16.</td>
  <td class="summary">God's wrath against them for their hypocrisy.</td>
