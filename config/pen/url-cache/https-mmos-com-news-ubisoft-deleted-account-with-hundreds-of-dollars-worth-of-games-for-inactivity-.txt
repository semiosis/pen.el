			<h1 class="entry-title post-title">Ubisoft Deleted Account With Hundreds Of Dollars Worth Of Games For Inactivity</h1>		
		
				<div class="entry-meta postmeta clearfix"><span class="meta-date sep"><a href="https://mmos.com/news/ubisoft-deleted-account-with-hundreds-of-dollars-worth-of-games-for-inactivity" title="6:06 am" rel="bookmark"><time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2021-12-20T06:06:03-08:00">December 20, 2021</time></a></span><span class="meta-author sep"> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="https://mmos.com/author/marcmarasigan" title="View all posts by Marc Marasigan" rel="author">Marc Marasigan</a></span></span>		
		
	<span class="meta-comments">
		<a href="https://mmos.com/news/ubisoft-deleted-account-with-hundreds-of-dollars-worth-of-games-for-inactivity#disqus_thread">Comments</a>

	</span>
	
</div>
		
		
		<div class="entry clearfix">
			<p><noscript><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-184762" src="https://mmos.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/04/ubisoft-assassins-creed-unity-notre-dame-banner.jpg" alt="ubisoft assassins creed notre dame" width="857" height="238" srcset="https://mmos.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/04/ubisoft-assassins-creed-unity-notre-dame-banner.jpg 857w, https://mmos.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/04/ubisoft-assassins-creed-unity-notre-dame-banner-300x83.jpg 300w, https://mmos.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/04/ubisoft-assassins-creed-unity-notre-dame-banner-768x213.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 857px) 100vw, 857px" /></noscript><img class="lazyload alignleft size-full wp-image-184762" src='data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20857%20238%22%3E%3C/svg%3E' data-src="https://mmos.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/04/ubisoft-assassins-creed-unity-notre-dame-banner.jpg" alt="ubisoft assassins creed notre dame" width="857" height="238" data-srcset="https://mmos.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/04/ubisoft-assassins-creed-unity-notre-dame-banner.jpg 857w, https://mmos.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/04/ubisoft-assassins-creed-unity-notre-dame-banner-300x83.jpg 300w, https://mmos.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/04/ubisoft-assassins-creed-unity-notre-dame-banner-768x213.jpg 768w" data-sizes="(max-width: 857px) 100vw, 857px" /></p>
<p>If you havent logged in to your Ubisoft account or UPlay in a while, you might want to check on it now. The French game publisher had apparently deleted an account for inactivity even when it had paid games in its library.</p>
<p>According to <a href="https://www.pcworld.com/article/559218/could-gdpr-policy-erase-your-games-it-happened-to-an-ubisoft-customer.html" rel="nofollow"  target="_blank">PCWorld</a>, the account in question had been inactive for more than a year when the owner decided to take a break from gaming. In 2020, I sold my PC because I was gaming way too much and it went a bit over the healthy way of doing it. I made a choice to work and attend school, said the account owner Tor.</p>
<p>When he decided to get back into gaming in summer 2021, he found that he was unable to login to his Ubisoft account. He was able to reset his password but later discovered that the account itself had been closed and permanently deleted along with hundreds of dollars worth of games. Tor added that none of his other game-related accounts had been closed, except for Ubisoft.</p>
<p>Upon checking his email, Tor said that he found an inactivity warning from Ubisoft in his spam folder dated January 20<sup>th</sup>. The email stated that his account had been temporarily shut down and will be permanently closed if he didnt click the provided link within 30 days.</p>
<p>When he tried contacting Ubisoft regarding the email, the companys representatives told him that they wont be able to recover any of his games because his account had been closed. If the account is closed, there is no way to restore it, said the support rep.</p>
<p>While theres a clause in Terms of Service that allows the company to suspend or close an account for inactivity, Ubisoft claims that what happened to Tor was a glitch and that theyve never suspended an account that has been inactive for less than four years. The company also insists that they would never close an account with purchased games in its library.</p>
<p>Unfortunately, theres also a clause in the ToS that no credit will be given back to the user in cases of account termination or suspension. The companys support page also cites the General Data Protection Regulation compliance and freeing database space as the main reason why they delete unused accounts.</p>
<p>Please be reassured that Ubisoft does not automatically close inactive accounts, reads the support page. However, per our Terms of Use, in rare instances we can take action to comply with some local data protection legislation. This is only if we have strong reasons to believe that the account in question will remain unused. We may also close long-term inactive accounts to maintain our database. You will be notified by email if we begin the process of closing your inactive account.</p>
<p>Ubisoft, however, says that Tors account doesnt fit the criteria for deletion and that they will be reaching out to him to conduct an investigation on the matter and take the appropriate measures.</p>
<p>As for how other video game companies and publishers handle their inactivity, Valve says that they do not deactivate inactive Steam accounts. Period. They also dont include them in active user statistics of data reports. Although <a href="https://www.reddit.com/r/pcgaming/comments/riru24/ubisoft_deletes_customers_account_with_paid_games/" rel="nofollow"  target="_blank">one Redditor</a> claimed that Valve deleted his Steam account which included several games and mods after 13 years of inactivity.</p>
<p>Epic Games ToS states that they may change the name of inactive accounts but says nothing about deleting them. Blizzards terms of service also doesnt mention account deletion due to inactivity.</p>
<p><a href="https://mmos.com/publishers/riot-games">Riot Games</a>, on the other hand, will suspend or terminate accounts if theyve been inactive for a prolonged period of time. While Riots portfolio doesnt include buy-to-play games, any DLCs or add-ons connected to the account will also be deleted upon account termination.</p>
<p>The crazy thing about this is that its perfectly legal for video game companies to delete your account regardless of whether youve spend zero or a thousand dollars on their games, as video game law attorney Ryan Morrison explained to <a href="https://www.pcworld.com/article/559218/could-gdpr-policy-erase-your-games-it-happened-to-an-ubisoft-customer.html" rel="nofollow"  target="_blank">PCWorld</a>.</p>
<p>They can delete your account for any reason they want, he said, expounding on the fact that users dont actually buy games from these companies, they license them. Using books as an example, Morrison explained that buying a physical book will grant you ownership of that book, while buying an ebook for Amazon Kindle will only buy you a license to display the book. He also added that Amazon can revoke that license whenever they want, as is the case with digital copies of video games.</p>
<p>As for me, I checked all my account and emails before writing this and thankfully theyre all there. Not that Ive been inactive for longer than a week or so, but I just wanted to make sure. Thoughts? Let us know in the comments section below.</p>
