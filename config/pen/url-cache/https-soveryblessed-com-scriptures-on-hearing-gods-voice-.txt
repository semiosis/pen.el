<p class="has-text-align-center"><strong>God speaks to us through His Word, but how do you really hear what He says? Learn how to hear and listen to God&rsquo;s voice.</strong></p>



<p>We serve a God who is personal and relational. Just like in any relationship communication is an important aspect through which we get to know each other, and how we remain connected. <strong>We speak to God through prayer, and though He can speak to us in different ways, the primary way God speaks to us today is through the Bible, His Holy Word.&nbsp;</strong></p>



<p>When you feel as though you haven&rsquo;t &ldquo;heard from God,&rdquo; open His Word. The Word is described in 2 Timothy 3:16-17, saying, &ldquo;All Scripture is God-breathed and is useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting and training in righteousness, so that the servant of God may be thoroughly equipped for every good work.&rdquo;</p>



<p>This means that <strong>God is speaking through His Word, it is the recorded words of God.</strong> How wonderful is it that we have access to His Word, and sometimes even in many different translations and forms.&nbsp;</p>



<p>These scriptures about hearing God&rsquo;s voice show how God has revealed Himself in history, and continues to speak to us today.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>1. </strong>Romans 10:17 &ndash; Faith Comes By Hearing the Message Of God&rsquo;s Word&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>Consequently, faith comes from hearing the message, and the message is heard through the word about Christ.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>2. </strong>John 10:27 &ndash; My Sheep Listen to My Voice&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>My sheep listen to my voice; I know them, and they follow me.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>3. </strong>Hebrews 3:15 &ndash; If You Hear His Voice Do Not Harden Your Hearts&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>As has just been said: &ldquo;Today, if you hear his voice, do not harden your hearts as you did in the rebellion.&rdquo;</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>4. </strong>2 Timothy 3:16-17 &ndash; All Scripture Is God-Breathed&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>All Scripture is God-breathed and is useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting and training in righteousness, so that the servant of God may be thoroughly equipped for every good work.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>5. </strong>Hebrews 1:1-2. &ndash; In these Last Days He Has Spoken To Us By His Son</h3>



<p><em>In the past God spoke to our ancestors through the prophets at many times and in various ways, but in these last days he has spoken to us by his Son, whom he appointed heir of all things, and through whom also he made the universe.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>6. </strong>Job 33:14 &ndash; God Does Speak&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>For God does speak &mdash; now one way, now another &mdash; though no one perceives it.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>7. </strong>John 8:47 &ndash; Whoever Belongs To God Hears What He Says&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>Whoever belongs to God hears what God says. The reason you do not hear is that you do not belong to God.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>8. </strong>1 Peter 3:12 &ndash; His Ears Are Attentive To The Prayer Of the Righteous</h3>



<p><em>For the eyes of the Lord are on the righteous and his ears are attentive to their prayer, but the face of the Lord is against those who do evil.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>9. </strong>Isaiah 30:21 &ndash; You Will Hear A Voice Behind You</h3>



<p><em>Whether you turn to the right or to the left, your ears will hear a voice behind you, saying, &ldquo;This is the way; walk in it.&rdquo;</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>10. </strong>Deuteronomy 4:12 &ndash; The Lord Spoke Out Of the Fire&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>Then the Lord spoke to you out of the fire. You heard the sound of words but saw no form; there was only a voice.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>11. </strong>Revelation 3:22 &ndash; Hear What the Spirit Says To the Churches&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>Whoever has ears, let them hear what the Spirit says to the churches.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>12. </strong>Psalm 85:8 &ndash; Listen To What God the Lord Says&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>I will listen to what God the Lord says; he promises peace to his people, his faithful servants &mdash; but let them not turn to folly.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>13. </strong>Hebrews 4:7 &ndash; If You Hear His Voice Do Not Harden Your Heart</h3>



<p><em>God again set a certain day, calling it &ldquo;Today.&rdquo; This he did when a long time later he spoke through David, as in the passage already quoted: &ldquo;Today, if you hear his voice, do not harden your hearts.&rdquo;</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>14. </strong>John 5:30 &ndash; I Judge Only As I Hear&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>By myself I can do nothing; I judge only as I hear, and my judgment is just, for I seek not to please myself but him who sent me.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>15. </strong>Luke 11:28 &ndash; Blessed Are Those Who Hear the Word of God and Obey It&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>He replied, &ldquo;Blessed rather are those who hear the word of God and obey it.&rdquo;</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>16. </strong>Revelation 3:20 &ndash; If Anyone Hears His Voice and Opens the Door</h3>



<p><em>Here I am! I stand at the door and knock. If anyone hears my voice and opens the door, I will come in and eat with that person, and they with me.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>17. </strong>Psalm 29:3 &ndash; The Voice Of The Lord Is Over the Waters&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>The voice of the Lord is over the waters; the God of glory thunders, the Lord thunders over the mighty waters.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>18. </strong>John 10:2-4 &ndash; The Sheep Listen To His Voice</h3>



<p><em>The one who enters by the gate is the shepherd of the sheep. The gatekeeper opens the gate for him, and the sheep listen to his voice.&nbsp;</em></p>



<p><em>He calls his own sheep by name and leads them out. When he has brought out all his own, he goes on ahead of them, and his sheep follow him because they know his voice.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>19. </strong>Psalm 5:3 &ndash; You Hear My Voice In the Morning&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>In the morning, Lord, you hear my voice in the morning I lay my requests before you and wait expectantly.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>20. </strong>John 9:31 &ndash; He Listens To the Godly Person Who Does His Will</h3>



<p><em>We know that God does not listen to sinners. He listens to the godly person who does his will.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>21. </strong>Isaiah 55:11 &ndash; His Word Does Not Return To Him Empty&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>So is my word that goes out from my mouth: It will not return to me empty, but will accomplish what I desire and achieve the purpose for which I sent it.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>22. </strong>Jeremiah 33:3 &ndash; I Will Answer You and Tell You Great Things&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>Call to me and I will answer you and tell you great and unsearchable things you do not know.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>23. </strong>Isaiah 41:13 &ndash; He Says To You, Do Not Fear; I Will Help You</h3>



<p><em>For I am the Lord your God who takes hold of your right hand and says to you, Do not fear; I will help you.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>24. </strong>Deuteronomy 13:4 &ndash; Keep His Commands and Obey Him</h3>



<p><em>It is the Lord your God you must follow, and him you must revere. Keep his commands and obey him; serve him and hold fast to him.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>25. </strong>Genesis 3:8 &ndash; They Heard the Sound of the Lord God&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>Then the man and his wife heard the sound of the Lord God as he was walking in the garden in the cool of the day, and they hid from the Lord God among the trees of the garden.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>26. </strong>Deuteronomy 28:1-2 &ndash; Carefully Follow All His Commands&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>&nbsp;If you fully obey the Lord your God and carefully follow all his commands I give you today, the Lord your God will set you high above all the nations on earth. All these blessings will come on you and accompany you if you obey the Lord your God.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>27. </strong>James 1:22 &ndash; Do Not Merely Listen To the Word&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>Do not merely listen to the word, and so deceive yourselves. Do what it says.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>28. </strong>Matthew 4:4 &ndash; Man Shall Live On Every Word That Comes From the Mouth of God&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>Jesus answered, &ldquo;It is written: &lsquo;Man shall not live on bread alone, but on every word that comes from the mouth of God.&rsquo;</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>29. </strong>Hebrews 4:2 &ndash; The Good News Proclaimed To Us&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>For we also have had the good news proclaimed to us, just as they did; but the message they heard was of no value to them, because they did not share the faith of those who obeyed.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>30. </strong>Matthew 7:24 &ndash; Hear His Words And Put Them Into Practice&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>Therefore everyone who hears these words of mine and puts them into practice is like a wise man who built his house on the rock.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>31. </strong>John 14:26 &ndash; The Holy Spirit Will Remind You Of Everything Jesus Has Said&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>But the Advocate, the Holy Spirit, whom the Father will send in my name, will teach you all things and will remind you of everything I have said to you.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>32. </strong>Luke 8:21 &ndash; Hearers and Doers Of the Word&nbsp;</h3>



<p><em>He replied, &ldquo;My mother and brothers are those who hear God&rsquo;s word and put it into practice.&rdquo;</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>33. </strong>Deuteronomy 5:27 &ndash; Listen To All That the Lord Our God Says</h3>



<p><em>Go near and listen to all that the Lord our God says. Then tell us whatever the Lord our God tells you. We will listen and obey.</em></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>34. </strong>Ezekiel 3:27 &ndash; This Is What the Sovereign Lord Says</h3>



<p><em>But when I speak to you, I will open your mouth and you shall say to them, &lsquo;This is what the Sovereign Lord says.&rsquo; Whoever will listen let them listen, and whoever will refuse let them refuse; for they are a rebellious people.</em></p>
