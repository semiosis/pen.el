<h1>&#8220;All Nations&#8221; Verse List</h1>
<p class="large"></p>
</div>
<p><em>The following Scripture references (New International Version) reveal God&#8217;s heart for all ethnic groups: &#8220;all nations,&#8221; &#8220;all peoples,” &#8220;all mankind,” &#8220;all creation,” “every creature,&#8221; &#8220;every knee,&#8221; &#8220;every tongue,&#8221; &#8220;every language,&#8221; and “the world.”</em></p>
<p><strong>Genesis 12:3</strong> &#8211; &#8220;I will bless those who bless you, and whoever curses you I will curse; and <strong>all peoples on earth</strong> will be blessed through you.&#8221;</p>
<p><strong>Genesis 18:18 </strong>&#8211; &#8220;Abraham will surely become a great and powerful nation, and <strong>all nations on earth</strong> will be blessed through him.&#8221;</p>
