                                <td rowspan="3" class="number">1.</td>
                                <td nowrap="nowrap">Q.</td>
                                <td><h2><div class="line">What is your only comfort</div>
<div class="line">in life and death?</div>
</h2></td>
                            </tr>
                            <tr>
                                <td nowrap="nowrap">A.</td>
                                <td><div class="line">That I am not my own, <sup><a rel="footnote" href="#fn-1-1">1</a></sup></div>
<div class="line indented">but belong with body and soul,</div>
<div class="line indented">both in life and in death, <sup><a rel="footnote" href="#fn-1-2">2</a></sup></div>
<div class="line indented">to my faithful Saviour Jesus Christ. <sup><a rel="footnote" href="#fn-1-3">3</a></sup></div>
<div class="line">He has fully paid for all my sins</div>
<div class="line indented">with his precious blood, <sup><a rel="footnote" href="#fn-1-4">4</a></sup></div>
<div class="line indented">and has set me free</div>
<div class="line indented">from all the power of the devil. <sup><a rel="footnote" href="#fn-1-5">5</a></sup></div>
<div class="line">He also preserves me in such a way <sup><a rel="footnote" href="#fn-1-6">6</a></sup></div>
<div class="line indented">that without the will of my heavenly Father</div>
<div class="line indented">not a hair can fall from my head; <sup><a rel="footnote" href="#fn-1-7">7</a></sup></div>
<div class="line indented">indeed, all things must work together</div>
<div class="line indented">for my salvation. <sup><a rel="footnote" href="#fn-1-8">8</a></sup></div>
<div class="line">Therefore, by his Holy Spirit</div>
<div class="line indented">he also assures me</div>
<div class="line indented">of eternal life <sup><a rel="footnote" href="#fn-1-9">9</a></sup></div>
<div class="line indented">and makes me heartily willing and ready</div>
<div class="line indented">from now on to live for him. <sup><a rel="footnote" href="#fn-1-10">10</a></sup></div>
</td>
                            </tr>
                            <tr>
                                <td></td>
                                <td class="ld-footnotes"><ul class="footnotes">
<li id="fn-1-1"><span class="number">1.</span> 1 Cor 6:19, 20.</li>
<li id="fn-1-2"><span class="number">2.</span> Rom 14:7-9.</li>
<li id="fn-1-3"><span class="number">3.</span> 1 Cor 3:23; Tit 2:14.</li>
<li id="fn-1-4"><span class="number">4.</span> 1 Pet 1:18, 19; 1 Jn 1:7; 2:2.</li>
<li id="fn-1-5"><span class="number">5.</span> Jn 8:34-36; Heb 2:14, 15; 1 Jn 3:8.</li>
<li id="fn-1-6"><span class="number">6.</span> Jn 6:39, 40; 10:27-30; 2 Thess 3:3; 1 Pet 1:5.</li>
<li id="fn-1-7"><span class="number">7.</span> Mt 10:29-31; Lk 21:16-18.</li>
<li id="fn-1-8"><span class="number">8.</span> Rom 8:28.</li>
<li id="fn-1-9"><span class="number">9.</span> Rom 8:15, 16; 2 Cor 1:21, 22; 5:5; Eph 1:13, 14.</li>
<li id="fn-1-10"><span class="number">10.</span> Rom 8:14.</li>
</ul></td>
                            </tr>
                        </table>
                    
                        <table class="question-and-answer">
                            <tr>
                                <td rowspan="3" class="number">2.</td>
                                <td nowrap="nowrap">Q.</td>
                                <td><h2><div class="line">What do you need to know</div>
<div class="line">in order to live and die</div>
<div class="line">in the joy of this comfort?</div>
</h2></td>
                            </tr>
                            <tr>
                                <td nowrap="nowrap">A.</td>
                                <td><div class="line">First,</div>
<div class="line indented">how great my sins and misery are; <sup><a rel="footnote" href="#fn-2-1">1</a></sup></div>
<div class="line">second,</div>
<div class="line indented">how I am delivered</div>
<div class="line indented">from all my sins and misery; <sup><a rel="footnote" href="#fn-2-2">2</a></sup></div>
<div class="line">third,</div>
<div class="line indented">how I am to be thankful to God</div>
<div class="line indented">for such deliverance. <sup><a rel="footnote" href="#fn-2-3">3</a></sup></div>
</td>
                            </tr>
                            <tr>
                                <td></td>
                                <td class="ld-footnotes"><ul class="footnotes">
<li id="fn-2-1"><span class="number">1.</span> Rom 3:9, 10; 1 Jn 1:10.</li>
<li id="fn-2-2"><span class="number">2.</span> Jn 17:3; Acts 4:12; 10:43.</li>
<li id="fn-2-3"><span class="number">3.</span> Mt 5:16; Rom 6:13; Eph 5:8-10; 1 Pet 2:9, 10.</li>
</ul></td>
                            </tr>
