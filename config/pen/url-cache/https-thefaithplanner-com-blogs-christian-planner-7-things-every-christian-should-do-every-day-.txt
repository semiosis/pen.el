          <p><span>Christians who follow Christ should live a life that is centered around Him. Christians are called to be disciples and to imitate Jesus in all aspects of their life. It's important for Christians to spend time with the Lord, read His Word, and spread the Gospel message every day. In this article, we will discuss 7 things that Christians should do everyday to live a Christ-centered life!</span></p>
<p><strong><span>What makes a good Christian? </span></strong></p>
<p><span>Christians should spend time with the Lord everyday. In order to have a Christ-centered life, Christians need to be in a close relationship with God. In fact, Christians are called "sons of light who walk as children of light" (Ephesians: Chapter five). That means Christians are supposed to imitate Jesus by spending time alone with God each day. We get our strength for living from him and he is able to guide us when we make decisions throughout the day. When you wake up in the morning, take some quiet time alone with your Bible or spiritual reading materials before starting your daily routine!</span></p>
<p><strong><span>How do I get started? </span></strong></p>
<p><span>It's simple! All Christians have to do is spend time alone each morning before they start their daily routine reading from the Bible or spiritual materials. You could even set an alarm on your phone for ten minutes every morning, so you don't forget this important step in developing your faith walk! You can use a <a href="https://thefaithplanner.com/">Christian planner</a> like <a href="https://thefaithplanner.com/">the Faith Planner</a> to help you track your daily Bible reading, prayer time, and other spiritual activities. Christians should also remember to pray for their family members every day!</span></p>
<p><strong><span>What are 7 things that Christians should do every day?</span></strong></p>
<ol>
<li><strong><span> Pray. </span></strong></li>
</ol>
<p><span>Christians should pray every day to God for their family, friends, and Christians around the world. Christians can ask God to help them be more like Christ and bring glory to Him through all the difficulties of life.</span></p>
<ol start="2">
<li><strong><span> Read the Bible.</span></strong></li>
</ol>
<p><span> Christians should read the Bible everyday. Christians can start their reading in the book of John and then move to another book before starting all over again! Christians are encouraged by God's Word since it brings us closer to Him. </span></p>
<ol start="3">
<li><strong><span> Practice random acts of kindness.</span></strong></li>
</ol>
<p><span> Christians should do acts of kindness for family, friends, Christians around the world, and even complete strangers. Christians can practice random acts of kindness through small things like buying groceries for a neighbor or giving water to someone who is thirsty!</span></p>
<ol start="4">
<li><strong><span> Confess your sins to God.</span></strong></li>
</ol>
<p><span>Christians should confess their sins to God every day. Christians can ask for forgiveness and receive it through the sacrifice of Jesus on the cross! Christians will feel a sense of relief after they have confessed their sin to God, knowing that He is faithful and just when He forgives us.</span></p>
<ol start="5">
<li><strong><span> Love others.</span></strong></li>
</ol>
<p><span>Christians should love others every day. Christians are supposed to imitate Jesus by loving everyone, even their enemies! Christians can show love through being kind and generous or doing things that they know will make other people smile.</span></p>
<ol start="6">
<li><strong><span> Share your faith.</span></strong></li>
</ol>
<p><span>Christians should share their faith every day. Christians are called to spread the Gospel message and be a witness for Jesus Christ! Christians can do this by praying regularly, going on mission trips, or talking about God's love with family members and friends.</span></p>
<p><span>Christians should spend time with God every day to strengthen their faith walk and be more like Christ. Christians can use a <a href="https://thefaithplanner.com/">Christian planner</a> like <a href="https://thefaithplanner.com/">the Faith Planner</a> to help them track what they do each day for God! They can choose what they want to practice and try their best every day!</span></p>
